Kathiyawadi

A Kathiyawadi thali typically includes a variety of mix vegetables dishes that are spicy and flavourful, and are known for their unique blend of sweet, sour, and spicy flavors. The thali may include dishes like "bhakri", "bajra ni rotla", "sev tameta nu shaak" , "ringan no olo", "undhiyu" , "sukhdi", and "chaas".

Gujarati

A typical Gujarati thali may include dishes like "dal", "kadhi", "undhiyu", "aamras", "farsan", and "rotli". It may also include "khichdi", "papad" and "chaas". Gujarati cuisine is known for its sweet, sour, and spicy flavors and the use of a variety of spices and herbs. The dishes are usually cooked with minimal oil and are light and easy to digest.
